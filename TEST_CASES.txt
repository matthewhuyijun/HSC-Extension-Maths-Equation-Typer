Test Cases for Left Delimiter Spacing Fix
===========================================

Paste these LaTeX expressions into the application to verify the fix:

1. Main fix - Partial derivative with \left( delimiter:
   \frac{\partial}{\partial x}\left(\frac{\partial u}{\partial x}\right)
   Expected: ∂/∂x (∂u/∂x)

2. Simple derivative with \left...\right:
   \frac{d}{dx}\left(x^2\right)
   Expected: d/dx (x^2)

3. Numeric fraction with \left...\right:
   \frac{1}{2}\left(a+b\right)
   Expected: 1/2 (a+b)

4. Fraction with \left| delimiter:
   \frac{dy}{dx}\left|_{x=0}\right.
   Expected: dy/dx |┬(x=0)

5. Heat equation (complex example):
   \frac{\partial u}{\partial t}=\alpha\frac{\partial^2 u}{\partial x^2}
   Expected: ∂u/∂t=α(∂^2 u)/(∂x^2)

All should have proper spacing after fractions!
